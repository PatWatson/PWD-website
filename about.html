About

Patrick Watson Design offers two services:

  • Logo Design
  • Brand Identity Design

The agency was founded by Patrick Watson, a graphic design student at Humber College in Toronto. Prior to design, he studied at Queen’s University in Kingston, graduating with a degree in Computer Engineering and worked at a large tech company.

With his Graphic Design Certificate and borrowing from his experience working in tech, Patrick takes an agile approach to each design project. The result is an efficient, iterative workflow that produces effective, innovative logos, brand identities and websites reflective of your company’s unique Customer Value Proposition.
