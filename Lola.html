Lola Tea

Specialty Tea Boutique


Lola sells specialty tea in six fun flavours through its three Ontario store locations and website.

A wordmark logo, responsive icon and packaging design was created for this project.

Lola's tone is happy, fun and whimsical to convey their brand message: "six healthy, fun tea flavours to keep you relaxed through out your busy day."
